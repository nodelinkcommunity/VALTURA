📋 Marketing Plan

Overview of all income types, eligibility conditions, and adjustable commission rates.

💡 Income Structure

Eligibility: Must hold an active Exclusive · VIP (360 Days) package to receive commissions & rewards.

Earnings Cap: Total income from ALL 5 sources combined cannot exceed 300% of Exclusive · VIP investment amount.

Forfeiture Rule: Commissions generated without an active Exclusive · VIP package are permanently lost.

⚡ Instant on Deposit

Calculated immediately when a VIP deposit (Signature or Exclusive) occurs in either leg.

Formula: min(Left VIP Volume, Right VIP Volume) × Rate%

Calculated Daily 00:00 UTC

Earned from daily profit of directly sponsored members (F1).

Formula: F1 Daily Profit × Rate%

Condition: Must have active Exclusive · VIP package. Counts toward 300% Earnings Cap.

Rate: 10%

Calculated Daily 00:00 UTC

Calculated on daily profit of the weaker leg (lower total ROI side).

Formula: Weak Leg Daily Profit × Rate%

Condition: Must have active Exclusive · VIP package. Counts toward 300% Earnings Cap.

Rate: 15%
